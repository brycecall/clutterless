Getting Started

This app is designed to be used in conjunction with the book, "The Life Changing Magic of Tidying Up" by Marie Kondo.

Use the categories and checklists to keep you focused, motivated, and on track during your Konmari Festival! This app will help ensure that you are thorough as you complete each category.

Once you have completed an item in a category, go ahead and check it off. Then, we'll keep track of your progress for you and let you know how far you've come.

Good luck on your journey to a more joy sparking life!
